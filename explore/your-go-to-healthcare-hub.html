Your Go-To Healthcare Hub

 

Why Choose Malaysia for Healthcare?

 

Malaysia is an excellent healthcare destination.

‘Malaysia Healthcare’ is a targeted superior-care programme that was launched in 2011 with the aim to create 'Quality Care for Your Peace of Mind’. This robust healthcare programme combines the efforts of the government, private hospitals, airlines, travel companies, and hotels working together to ensure that your healthcare journey is a seamless and worry-free process.

Over the years, Malaysia has evolved from its humble beginnings into a shining star, earning prestigious accolades at the Asia Pacific Healthcare & Medical Tourism Awards in 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2021. More recently, the country's healthcare system has even more reason to celebrate, having claimed the coveted Global Health Asia Pacific Healthcare & Medical Tourism Awards in both 2022 and 2023.
